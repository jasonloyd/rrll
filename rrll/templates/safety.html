USA Baseball Online Training Information

You will have to register on the site in order to take the courses (no fee).

Each course can generally take between 15minutes and 1 hour to complete.

Required:

  • Abuse Awareness for Adults

Highly Recommended:

  • Basic First Aid
  • Baserunning Fundamentals
  • Coaching the Basics of Hitting
  • Coaching the Fundamentals of Infield
  • Coaching the Fundamentals of Outfield
  • Introduction to Pitch Smart (not required for T-Ball or Rookie level)

Due to their training and education, it is not necessary for licensed medical doctors, licensed registered nurses, licensed practical nurses and paramedics to attend first aid training in order to meet requirement.

Other individuals who attend various outside first aid training and courses are not exempt.
