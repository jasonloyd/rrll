Residency Requirements

Players must reside or attend school inside the boundaries of Roadrunner Little League. You can check your address on League Finder to confirm your league.

Additional information on residency requirements is available from Little League International.

School Based Residency

Residency based on school attendance must be verified with the School Enrollment Form and must be signed by a school administrator.

Please complete all fields EXCEPT the administrator's name, signature, and title before presenting to the school, and have the form signed prior to registration.

RRLL league number is 04310808 and is needed for the form.
